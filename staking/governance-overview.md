Orca is a DAO, with powers delegated to a [DAO council](/staking/dao-council), established through a vote. Holders of the ORCA token can engage with [governance](/staking/governance-how-to): formulate, discuss and refine proposals and help plot Orca DAO's course through the DeFi seas.
