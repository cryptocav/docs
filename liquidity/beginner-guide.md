At first glance, navigating Orca’s CLMM may seem challenging for liquidity providers (LPs) accustomed to the Constant Product Market Maker (CPMM) model. However, with Orca v2, this transition is intuitive and seamless.
This guide is designed to offer users *a helping flipper* as they learn to navigate Orca’s v2 UI efficiently.
