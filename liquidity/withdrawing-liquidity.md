#### Open the Position Details sidebar

‚Üí See the [Position Details Sidebar Guide](/liquidity/position-sidebar) for a full walkthrough.

#### Enter Withdrawal amounts

With the `Withdraw` tab selected, enter the amount you wish to withdraw in one of the highlighted fields, or use the slider to select a portion of your position.

![](/images/image_94.jpg)

The other values will adjust automatically to match the withdrawal ratio for your current price range.

Alternatively, you can click `Max` in a field to withdraw the maximum quantity of tokens.

![](/images/image_95.jpg)

#### (Optional) Adjust your liquidity slippage

If required, adjust your slippage tolerance by clicking the `Liq. slippage` button.
‚Üí See [Understanding Slippage](/reference/educational-documents/understanding-slippage) for more information.

#### Complete your withdrawal

Once you are satisfied with your withdrawal amounts, you can click `Withdraw`.

![](/images/image_96.jpg)

If you enter values that result in removing all liquidity, or select `Max` in a token field, the `Withdraw` button will change to `Close Position`.

![](/images/image_97.jpg)

#### (Optional) Retain Your Pool Position NFT

If you are removing all your liquidity, you can choose to retain the NFT by unchecking the `Harvest and burn the NFT of this position` checkbox.

![](/images/image_98.jpg)

Retaining your NFT allows you to redeposit into the same empty range later, maintaining the original parameters.
