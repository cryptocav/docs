Close your liquidity position and withdraw all funds from your Eclipse position.

In addition to closing a position using the [*withdraw liquidity*](/liquidity/eclipse/eclipse-withdrawing-liquidity) function, a position can be closed directly using the *Close Position* function

* Navigate to your portfolio (https://www.orca.so/portfolio)
* Click on the ellipsis button (`...`) to the right of your position.
* Select `Ã— Close Position`.

   ![](/images/image_128.jpg)
* Check the displayed details, then click `Close Position`.

   ![](/images/image_129.jpg)
* Review details in your wallet and confirm.
