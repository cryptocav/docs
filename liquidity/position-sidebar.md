The *Position Details* sidebar incorporates many portfolio management functions in one place, allowing you to quickly monitor and manage your positions. The sidebar can be opened from two places

* The My Positions pane in Liqudiity Terminal
   → See [Liquidity Terminal Overview](/liquidity/terminal-overview).
* The [Portfolio page](https://www.orca.so/portfolio)

#### Accessing the sidebar

In both locations the method for opening the sidebar is the same, with two choices

* Click anywhere on your position

   ![](/images/image_69.jpg)
* Use the ellipsis button

   * Click on the ellipsis button ( ... ) to the right of your position

      ![](/images/image_70.jpg)
   * Select `Position Details`, `+ Deposit Liquidity`, or `- Withdraw Liquidity`.

      ![](/images/image_71.jpg)

#### Using the sidebar

Once the sidebar is open you will see that there are three tabs:

* `Details`: displays information about your position and its performance.

The `Details`tab also includes the option to `Harvest Yield`, for the selected position:

→ See the [How to Harvest Yield Guide](/liquidity/harvesting-yield)

`Deposit` lets you add additional liquidity to your position

→ See the [How to Add Liquidity Guide](/liquidity/adding-liquidity)

`Withdraw` lets you withdraw liquidity from your position

→ See the [How to Withdraw Liquidity Guide](/liquidity/withdrawing-liquidity)
