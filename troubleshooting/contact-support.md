Orca is committed to delivering first-class support and offers several channels for ticket assistance. You can reach us through [Discord](https://discord.gg/deRXbXWXk8) or [Telegram](https://t.me/orca_so/), but for the most convenient and secure experience, we recommend opening a support ticket directly on the [Orca's website](https://www.orca.so/).

### How to open a support ticket in Orca's UI

#### Navigate to [https://www.orca.so](https://www.orca.so/)

#### Connect your wallet

#### Click on the Support Button

**Desktop:**
• Click the `Support` button in the bottom-right corner of the screen, or
• Click your wallet icon in the top-right corner and select `Support`.

**Mobile:**
• Tap your wallet address in the top-right corner and choose `Support`.

![](/images/image_194.jpg)

### Sign a Message

To confirm ownership of your wallet you must sign a transaction
Using the built-in ticket system helps us verify your identity and ensures your request is routed to the right specialist as quickly as possible.

![](/images/image_195.jpg)

### Open a ticket

Click on `Start a New Chat`

![](/images/image_196.jpg)

### Create your ticket

Fill in the form providing as much relevant information as possible then click `Send`

![](/images/image_197.jpg)

### Wait for a reply

We strive to reply promptly; once a response is available, you’ll see it in `My Chat`

![](/images/image_198.jpg)

Thank you for using Orca—your satisfaction matters to us, and we’re here to help every step of the way.
